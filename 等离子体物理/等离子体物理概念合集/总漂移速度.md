曲率漂移：$$v_{curv}=\frac{mv_{\parallel}^{2}}{q} \vec{B}\times\frac{(\hat{b\cdot \nabla })\hat{b}}{B^2} $$
梯度漂移：
$$v_{\nabla B} = \frac{m v_{\perp}^2}{2q} \frac{\vec{R}_c \times \vec{B}}{R_c^2 B^2}$$
相加得到总漂移：$$v_{total} = \frac{1}{q} \left( m v_{\parallel}^2 + \frac{1}{2} m v_{\perp}^2 \right) \frac{\vec{R}_c \times \vec{B}}{R_c^2 B^2}$$
