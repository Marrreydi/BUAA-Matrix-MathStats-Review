## 常用：
$E\times B$漂移：$$v_{E}=\frac{E\times B}{B^2}$$
极化漂移：$$v_{P}=\frac{1}{\omega_{g}B}\frac{dE_{\perp}}{dt};j_{P}=\frac{n_{e}(m_{i}+m_{e})}{B^2}\frac{dE_{\perp}}{dt}$$
梯度漂移：$$v_{\nabla}=\frac{mv_{\perp}^2}{2qB^3}(\vec{B}\times \nabla B);j_{\nabla}=\frac{n_{e}(\mu_{i}+\mu_{e})}{B^2}(\vec{B}\times \nabla B)$$
曲率漂移：$$v_{R}=\frac{mv_{\parallel}^2}{qR_{c}^2B^2}(\vec{R}_{c}\times \vec{B});j_{R}=\frac{2n_{e}(W_{i\parallel}+W_{e\parallel})}{R^2B^2}(\vec{R}_{c}\times \vec{B})$$
